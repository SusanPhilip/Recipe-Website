EASY VEGETARIAN LASAGNA


Ingredients

  1. 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  2. 1 pound ground beef chuck
  3. 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  4. 1/2 large bell pepper (green, red, or yellow), diced (about 3/4 cup)
  5. 1 (28-ounce)can good-quality tomato sauce
  6. 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  7. 1 1/2 pounds (24 ounces) mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  8. 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles (requires 9 lasagna noodles - unbroken)
  9. 1/4 pound (4 ounces) freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  10. Salt

Method

  1. Step 1
    Start by making the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo
    of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes. The three kinds of
    tomatoes gives the sauce great depth of flavor.
  2. Step 2
    Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready. We're
    using ricotta, shredded mozzarella, and parmesan -- like the mix of tomatoes,
    this 3-cheese blend gives the lasagna great flavor!
  3. Step 3
    From there, it's just an assembly job. A cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles, more sauce, followed by a layer of cheese. Repeat until you have three layers
    and have used up all the ingredients. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!
