LEMON RICE


Ingredients

  1. 1 cup white rice
  2. 2 tablespoon cooking oil
  3. 1 tablespoon chana dal (split chickpea lentils)
  4. 1 tablespoon urad dal (split skinless black gram lentils)
  5. 2 tablespoons white sugar
  6. Â½ teaspoon black mustard seeds
  7. 2 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  8. 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  9. 10-12 fresh curry leaves
  10. Sea salt, to taste

Method

  1. Rinse the rice in a colander until the water runs clear. Drain and soak it in 1.25 cups water. Grate the ginger.
  2. Press the saute function, normal (medium heat), and add the oil. Tip in the mustard seeds and let them crackle.
  3. Add peanuts, chana dal and urad dal and fry until they turn golden. Add grated ginger, green chillies, curry leaves, turmeric and half cup water. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the rice and the water in which it was soaked, lemon juice and salt. Mix everything with a spoon and cancel saute.
  5. Close the lid and press the pressure cooking button (high pressure). Set the timer to 4 minutes. When the timer beeps, wait for 10 minutes, then release pressure
    and open the pot. Perfectly steamed easy lemon rice is ready to be served.
